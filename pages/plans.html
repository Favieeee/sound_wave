FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel during a free trial, you won't be charged. If you cancel a paid plan, you'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.
What does Hi-Fi audio actually mean? +
Hi-Fi (High Fidelity) means we stream music in lossless quality — up to 24-bit, 192kHz — which preserves every detail of the original recording. Standard streaming compresses audio and loses some of that detail. To fully appreciate Hi-Fi, you'll want a good pair of headphones or speakers.
How does the Family plan work? +
The Family plan lets you add up to 6 individual accounts under one subscription. Each member gets their own separate listening history, personalized recommendations, and download library. The account owner manages billing and can set parental controls for younger family members.
Is there a student discount? +
Yes! Students with a valid university email address can get the Pro plan for 50% off — just $4.99/month. You'll need to verify your student status through our partner SheerID. The discount applies for up to 4 years.
Can I use Soundwave on multiple devices? +
Yes. You can install Soundwave on as many devices as you like and your library syncs across all of them. However, you can only stream on one device at a time on the Free and Pro plans. The Family plan allows simultaneous streams across all 6 member accounts.
